E-Scraper

This is an E-commerce website scraper which scrapes the price details from various websites[amazon, flipkart, snapdeal, shopclues] and stores in a database. The urls to scrape has to be stored in the database for scraping.

This scraper is used to compare the price details of a particular product from various stores.
